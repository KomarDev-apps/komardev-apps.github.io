Privacy & Technical Transparency

We respect the professional nature of your work. PLC operates with a local-first data policy.

  • Data Handling: All MVR/CSV file processing and patch calculations are performed locally. Your project data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Camera Permission: Used only for the built-in Flashlight utility, a must-have for every tech in dark environments.
  • Internet: Used exclusively to download fixture libraries or MVR files from the cloud. No user-specific data is collected.
  • Cookies: This website is a static landing page and does not use any cookies or tracking scripts.
